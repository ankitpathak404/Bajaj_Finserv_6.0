A1: The policy covers hospitalization expenses including room rent, ICU/ICCU charges, medical practitioner fees, operation theatre costs, and medical supplies. Room rent limits are 1% of the sum insured (below Rs. 5 Lacs) or 1% of sum insured/standard AC room charges (Rs. 5 Lacs and above). ICU charges are 2% of sum insured per day (below Rs. 5 Lacs) or actuals (Rs. 5 Lacs and above).

Q3: What are the benefits and limits of Ambulance Services?
A3: The benefits and limits for Ambulance Services are up to 0.5% of the Sum Insured (max Rs. 2500 per event) and 1% of the Sum Insured (max Rs. 5000 per policy period).
--------------------------------------------------
Q4: What are the benefits and limits of telemedicine and maternity coverage under this policy?
A4: The policy does not provide coverage for telemedicine. For maternity coverage, the benefits are limited to 10% of the Sum Insured, with a maximum of Rs. 40,000 for normal delivery and Rs. 60,000 for caesarean section. Coverage for newborns beyond 90 days requires additional premium, and certain exclusions apply such as surrogate pregnancy and voluntary termination of pregnancy.
--------------------------------------------------
